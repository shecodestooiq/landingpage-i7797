BMW ..
The models of the BMW M4 GT3 and the BMW M Hybrid V8 from the LEGO® Speed Champions series impress with details such as wing mirrors, air intakes and characteristic kidney grills, based on the originals. Thanks to a removable roof, the miniatures of the racing drivers can be placed in the vehicles so that nothing stands in the way of exciting races in your own four walls.
